Questions

We've got answers.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

Not at all. If you can send an email, you can use AI. We start from zero and build up together. Most attendees have never used AI tools before — that's exactly who this is designed for.

What do I need to bring?

A laptop (charged) and a free ChatGPT account. That's it. We'll send setup instructions before the event so you're ready to go on day one.

Is lunch included?

Coffee, tea, and snacks are provided throughout the day. For lunch (12–1 PM), the hotel has dining available nearby. Or stay for the optional bonus live AI demo during the lunch hour.

What if I can't make it?

Tickets are non-refundable but transferable. You can send someone else in your place — just let us know in advance.

What happens after the workshop?

You get 30 days free in our AI Business Lab community — ongoing support, weekly office hours, and new resources dropped regularly. After that, it's $97/month if you choose to stay.

How many people will be there?

We cap it at 25 so everyone gets personal attention. This isn't a lecture hall — it's a working session where you'll actually build things alongside people who run businesses like yours.
